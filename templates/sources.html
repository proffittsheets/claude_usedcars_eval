Sources & Attributions

Data provenance, image credits, and terms of use for all third-party content

About This Site

This is a personal research project created by Molly & Justin Sheets to help evaluate new and recent vehicle models before purchasing a used car. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any automaker, dealership, rating agency, or media outlet referenced on this site.

All content is for personal, non-commercial research purposes only. Pricing shown as "est." reflects estimates based on prior-year figures and should not be relied on for purchase decisions. Safety ratings and fuel economy data are reproduced from public government sources and may not reflect the most current model year.

Safety Ratings

NHTSA 5-Star Safety Ratings

Overall crash safety ratings (1–5 stars) for front crash, side crash, and rollover resistance.

  • Provider: National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (U.S. Department of Transportation)
  • API: api.nhtsa.gov/SafetyRatings/
  • License: U.S. Government data, public domain — no restrictions on use
  • Terms: NHTSA Web Policies

IIHS Top Safety Pick & Top Safety Pick+

TSP and TSP+ award status is manually curated from the IIHS website and stored in data/manual/iihs_overrides.json. IIHS does not offer a public API. 2026 awards have not yet been announced; those entries are shown as "Not awarded."

  • Provider: Insurance Institute for Highway Safety (IIHS)
  • Source page: iihs.org/ratings/top-safety-picks
  • License: Reproduced for personal research; IIHS retains all rights
  • Terms: iihs.org/about/terms-of-use

Fuel Economy

FuelEconomy.gov Web Services

City, highway, and combined MPG figures; fuel type; hybrid/PHEV classification.

  • Provider: U.S. Department of Energy / U.S. EPA
  • API: fueleconomy.gov/feg/ws/index.shtml
  • License: U.S. Government data, public domain — no restrictions on use

Vehicle Images

Toyota Motor North America Pressroom

Official press photography for Toyota vehicles. Images are year-specific and hosted on Toyota's Amazon S3 media infrastructure.

  • Provider: Toyota Motor North America
  • Source: pressroom.toyota.com
  • License: Toyota press images — reproduced for personal research only. All rights reserved by Toyota Motor North America.
  • Terms: pressroom.toyota.com/terms-of-use/

Motor Trend — Nissan, Mercedes-Benz, Lincoln

Year-specific vehicle photography for Nissan, Mercedes-Benz, and Lincoln models sourced from Motor Trend editorial pages. Images are served from the Hearst CDN (hips.hearstapps.com/mtg-prod/) and include the model year in the filename.

  • Provider: Motor Trend Group / Hearst Autos
  • Source: motortrend.com
  • License: Hearst editorial photography — reproduced for personal research only. All rights reserved by Hearst Autos.
  • Terms: motortrend.com/terms/

Wikimedia Commons

Community-contributed vehicle photos used as a fallback where manufacturer press sources do not provide coverage for a given model year. Each image is attributed individually in data/raw/images/manifest.json. Licenses vary by image; CC BY-SA 4.0 is most common.

  • Provider: Wikimedia Foundation / individual contributors
  • API: commons.wikimedia.org/w/api.php
  • License: Creative Commons (varies per image — CC BY-SA 4.0 most common). Attribution stored in manifest.
  • Terms: Wikimedia Terms of Use

Vehicle Specifications

CarQuery API

Body type, drivetrain, and seat count data. The CarQuery API has been intermittently unavailable since early April 2026; affected fields may show as blank.

  • Provider: CarQuery (carqueryapi.com)
  • API: carqueryapi.com/api/0.3/
  • License: Free for personal and commercial use per CarQuery terms

Pricing (MSRP)

Manufacturer Configurator Pages

Base MSRP figures are manually entered from each brand's official vehicle configurator. Prices marked "est." are estimates only (~2–3% increase from the prior model year) and have not been confirmed by the manufacturer for the 2026 model year.

  • Sources: toyota.com, nissanusa.com, mbusa.com, lincoln.com
  • License: Reproduced for personal reference only
